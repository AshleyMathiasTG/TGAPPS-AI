Job Description: Jira & Confluence Operational Support Admin
Position Overview
We are seeking a dedicated Jira and Confluence Operational Support Admin to provide
day-to-day operational support for our Atlassian tools.
The ideal candidate will be responsible for supporting user issues, managing roles and
access, provisioning project spaces, and handling L1 and L2 incidents related to Jira and
Confluence.
Key Responsibilities
• Operational Support
• Provide timely support for Jira and Confluence user issues and queries.
• Troubleshoot and resolve L1 (basic) and L2 (intermediate) incidents related
to Jira and Confluence platforms.
• Escalate complex issues to relevant teams as required.
• User & Access Management
• Create, modify, and manage user roles and permissions within Jira and
Confluence.
• Ensure appropriate access controls are maintained in accordance with
organizational policies.
• Project Space Provisioning
• Set up and configure new project spaces in Jira and Confluence per user or
team requests.
• Maintain documentation and templates for efficient project space creation.
• Incident Handling
• Monitor and respond to incident tickets for Jira and Confluence.
• Perform root cause analysis for recurring issues and recommend preventive
actions.
• Collaboration & Documentation
• Collaborate with IT and other support teams for cross-platform issues.
• Maintain clear documentation of processes, incidents, and resolutions.
Requirements
• Experience: 3-5 years of hands-on experience administering Jira and Confluence.
• Technical Skills:
• Proficiency in Jira and Confluence administration (user management,
permissions, project space setup).
• Basic understanding of incident management workflows.
• Familiarity with Atlassian best practices.
• Soft Skills:
• problem-solving and communication skills.
• Ability to prioritize and manage multiple tasks simultaneously.
• Customer-focused attitude.
Preferred Qualifications
• Experience with ITSM tools and processes.
• Atlassian certifications (Jira/Confluence Administrator) are a plus.
