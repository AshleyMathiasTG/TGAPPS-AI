Job Description: SAP BASIS lead Consultant (10-12 Years Experience)
Position Summary
We are seeking an experienced SAP BASIS lead Consultant with 10-12 years of hands-on experience in SAP system administration, SOX audit compliance, and S/4HANA Cloud migration activities. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record in managing complex SAP landscapes, ensuring high system availability, supporting audit and security requirements, and leading technical migration and transformation initiatives.
Key Responsibilities
SAP BASIS Administration lead
Manage end-to-end SAP BASIS operations across SAP ECC, S/4HANA, BW, PO/PI, Solution Manager, GRC, and related components.
Perform system monitoring, performance tuning, kernel upgrades, transport management, job administration, and system refreshes.
Ensure high availability, scalability, and stability of SAP systems across development, QA, and production environments.
Maintain Dev/QA/UAT support landscape integrated with Salesforce, MES and other enterprise systems, Plan and build project landscape as needed for large project.
Provide singular support point for infra issues to the functional team by developing an understanding of how server, network and firewalls are set up, experience in handling P1/P2 situations by working through the teams
Monitor batch job, reruns based on the rerun documentation.
Ensure 24x7 on-call coverage between 3 Basis leads for off India hours and to cover absence of US onsite administration
Experience in IBP implementation
Experience in SAP integrations and BTP set up
Peer review of Basis changes
SOX Audit & Compliance
Lead and support SAP technical compliance activities aligned with SOX controls and corporate governance requirements.
Prepare required documentation, evidence, and reports for internal and external audits.
Implement and enforce SAP security standards, access controls, and change management policies.
Collaborate with Security, Internal Controls, and Risk teams to ensure ongoing compliance.
S/4HANA Cloud Migration
Lead or support SAP S/4HANA Cloud migration activities, including system readiness, technical assessments, and cutover planning.
Coordinate and execute migration tasks such as system provisioning, integration setup, data migration support, interface validations, and performance optimization.
Work closely with functional, basis, and cloud platform teams to ensure smooth transition and post-migration stabilization.
Technical Expertise
Strong hands-on experience with SAP HANA administration, performance tuning, backup and recovery, and system replication.
Understanding of SAP Cloud ALM, SAP BTP, and cloud architecture (Azure/AWS/GCP) is preferred.
Experience with SAP Support Packs, OSS notes, and system maintenance activities.
Strong knowledge of SAP transport landscapes, ChaRM, and change management processes.

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field.
10-12 years of experience as an SAP BASIS administrator in large, complex SAP environments.
Proven experience handling SOX audit processes and technical compliance requirements.
Experience in SAP S/4HANA Cloud migration or conversion projects.
Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with the ability to troubleshoot complex technical issues.
Excellent communication, documentation, and stakeholder management abilities.
Ability to work independently and lead technical initiatives.
